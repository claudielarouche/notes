SEO Checklist

[camelCaseWordsEnabled: false]

 I have investigated all options for improving the reach of my webpage.
 I have confirmed that my page’s keywords reflect the keywords that users frequently use.
 I have confirmed that the page is a content page (and not a menu page or a links page).
 I have permission/authority to make changes to the page content or I have contacted the page owner.
 I have consulted with IT to ensure the page is coded correctly (including multimedia elements).
 I have implemented the SEO guidelines as outlined on GCPedia (see “SEO Resources” below).
 I have verified that the page’s redirects (if used) have been done properly (i.e. are not cascading).
 I have confirmed that the page attracts the intended audience
 I have added links pointing to this page.
