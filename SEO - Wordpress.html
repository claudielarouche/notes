SEO - Wordpress

[camelCaseWordsEnabled: false]

Install the plugin Wordpress SEO by Yoast

Under General Settings, enter Webmaster Tools information

Under titles and meta, don't make changes to the General page. There is a list of macros under Help.

Under media, click Add Facebook Admin and fill rest of the information for Facebook, Twitter and Google+

If Social Media features are configured properly, it's a powerful option to bring in more people to your site

Under permalink, Redirect attachment URL's to parent post URL will send people to the post if they found the image somewhere and clicked on it (instead of them seeing ONLY the image). Strip Category is ok to change too but all the other settings on this page may mess up your website so use with caution.

Edit Files: Don't touch it :)

Now when you create a new post or page, you have new options at the bottom of the page. Social tab is interesting, you can create a custom description of your post just for Facebook or just for Google+

Focus keyword: As you type, plugin will give you suggestions. As soon as you enter those keywords, SEO plugin will tell you where the keywords are missing.

After you fill in your focus keyword and add it to various places on your post, click on Page Analysis and review advices.
